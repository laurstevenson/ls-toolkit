Microsoft Bookings

Why Microsoft (MS) Bookings?

In theme with the Ventus updates that are meant to empower students to select academic accommodations per course, we will carry out a similar strategy for schedule appointments with Learning Specialists. We've discussed using Microsoft Bookings in the past, now it's time to put the plan into action!

It is important to note that students will only see your Bookings calendar. They will not see your Outlook calendars unless you specifically authorize them access to it.

MS Bookings, Outlook, and MS Teams work simultaneously. However, there are important steps that can only be done using MS Bookings. This guide is meant to help you get familiar with the ins and outs of Bookings. Topics covered include:

  • Getting started: How to access your MS Bookings Calendar/Page
  • What your students will see
  • What happens when a student books an appointment?
  • Cancelling an appointment
  • FAQ

Getting Started

To log in to MS Bookings, type in the url book.ms in your browser and sign in using your UO credentials. You'll be redirected to your MS Bookings Welcome page. Access your Calendar.

Note: You can also see your scheduled meetings in Outlook. However, the Bookings Calendar view will accurately show your appointment and buffer times, so it's important that you don't rely too much on Outlook for these meetings.

You must add summer hours, personal time off, federal/provincial holidays, and 15-minute break times in Outlook. Your calendars already include the following:

  • Work hours
  • Lunch break
  • AM doc time
  • On call hours

What your students will see

Students will be able to select a service (appointment type), date and time based on your configured availability.

What happens when a student books an appointment?

  • An email confirmation is sent to both the student and the staff member booked. The student's confirmation email also includes a link to modify or cancel the appointment
  • A calendar entry is created into your MS Bookings page and Outlook calendar
  • A MS Teams join link will be included for the student to follow at the appointment time. The MS Teams information is also included in your Outlook calendar and MS Bookings page

Schedule an appointment

  • To schedule a single appointment through your MS Bookings page, click on New Booking and select the appointment type
  • Enter the student's information in the Customer name, customer email, phone number and student number fields
  • Select a date and start time for the appointment. Your 15-minute buffer time will automatically be applied at the end
  • Don't forget to click Save!

Cancel an appointment

  • In your MS Bookings calendar page, find and select the appointment you wish to cancel
  • Select Cancel and provide a reason for cancellation to the student
  • Lastly, click on Cancel Booking, this will notify the student via email and remove the entry from your calendar
