Following your initial meeting with the student, take a new minutes to finish yourPoint of Contact (POC) in Ventus:

  • Briefly describe the point of discussion as well as the outcome of your meeeting. Which accommodations did you establish and why? What is the duration of the accommodation plan? Did you provide the student with any letters of support?
  • Did you discuss a learning strategy plan? Referrals to campus resources?

Once complete, don't forget to lock your POC! Remember, once you lock your POC you cannot modify its content. However, you could always append the interaction.

Complete any follow-up actions as agreed or needed. Do you need to consult with the exams team? Do you have to submit a referral to the Adapted Technology Centre?

Lastly, change the event status of the student's appointment to Completed on your Ventus calar. To do so, click on the student's appointment, event status and select follow up complete
