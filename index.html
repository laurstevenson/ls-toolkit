As a Learning Specialist, your role and responsibilities are essential to the Academic Accommodation process.

  • Evaluate disability documentation to carefully consider the functional limitations
  • Assess needs which may impact the academic or campus experience.
  • Establish a reasonable and individualized academic accommodations plan which may include: Exam accommodations, Teaching accommodations, Transcriptions accommodations and Learning strategies.
  • Offer learning strategies to build and improve academic skills based on challenges and strengths.
  • Assist students, professors and other support staff to ensure that approved accommodations are implemented, while maintaining confidentiality.
  • Providing student with the pertinent information about services and supports available.
